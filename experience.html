Vibration Analysis

At Arup, I have used accelerometers to measure both footfall and rail vibrations. Although these vibrations are rarely a safety problem, vibrations can be a source of disturbance both to people and to senstive equipment. By measuring the current or expected vibrations, we analyze the causes of the vibration and recommend remediation or design measures.
